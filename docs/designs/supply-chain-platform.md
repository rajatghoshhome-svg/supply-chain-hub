## Vision

### 10x Check
The Autonomous Supply Chain Brain: instead of a planner running the system and reviewing exceptions, the system runs itself. It monitors incoming orders, detects demand signals in real-time, triggers planning cascades automatically, resolves routine exceptions without human input, and only escalates genuinely novel situations. Self-learning exception resolution where every customer interaction makes the system smarter. Supplier intelligence that pre-positions inventory before problems occur. Financial impact on every decision so the CFO can see ROI in real-time.

### Platonic Ideal
The system feels like having a senior planning director sitting next to you 24/7. You open the app in the morning and see: "Here's what happened overnight. Three things need your attention. Two things I already handled. One thing I'm not sure about — here are my options and my recommendation." The interface is calm, not 47 flashing alerts. Drill into any module and see the reasoning chain. Override anything with one click. The AI doesn't argue, it shows what changes if you override, and learns your preference.
